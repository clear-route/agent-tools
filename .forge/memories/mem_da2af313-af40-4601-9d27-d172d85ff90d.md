The outlook-assistant `mail/formatting.go` supports three body formats via `--format` flag: `text` (plain text → HTML with `<br>` conversion), `md` (Markdown → HTML with full rendering), `html` (pass-through HTML). The `toHTMLBody` heuristic (detect leading `<`) and `textToHTML` (escape + `<br>`) helpers have been removed. `RenderBody` is now the single entry point for all body formatting.
