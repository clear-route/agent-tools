The `excalidraw-assistant` tool in `clear-route/agent-tools` uses `entrypoint: excalidraw-assistant.go` in `tool.yaml` (the Go source file, not a compiled binary). Forge compiles/runs `.go` entrypoints directly. The compiled binary and Python `.venv` live only in `~/.forge/tools/excalidraw-assistant/` after a build/install step — not in the monorepo.
