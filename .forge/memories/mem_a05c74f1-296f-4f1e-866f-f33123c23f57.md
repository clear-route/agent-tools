For `clear-route/agent-tools` excalidraw-assistant: the source repo `tool.yaml` keeps `entrypoint: excalidraw-assistant.go`; the deployed `~/.forge/tools/excalidraw-assistant/tool.yaml` must have `entrypoint: excalidraw-assistant` (compiled binary). These two files intentionally differ. The build/deploy step is: `cd excalidraw-assistant && go build -o ~/.forge/tools/excalidraw-assistant/excalidraw-assistant excalidraw-assistant.go`, then copy `tool.yaml` and `README.md` to `~/.forge/tools/excalidraw-assistant/`, then copy/sync `references/` directory.
